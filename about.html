About Us

Company

Established in 2013 as a sole trader in the Northern Territory, KP Building Approvals has since become a nationwide propriety limited company with offices servicing the Sunshine Coast, South Brisbane and the Northern Territory.
As a medium sized company we pride ourselves with the professionalism and quality of a large organization but with the direct and personal relationship of an individual professional desired by our clients.

Capabilities

We provide a full range of certification and consultation services for commercial and domestic buildings of any size to the whole Northern Territory and Queensland.
We are Professional and proactive; our experience and resources will provide you with significant savings and prompt customized services.
We are proud of our reputation and strongly believe “Your dream is our Priority”.
